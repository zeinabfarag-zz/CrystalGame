Crystal Collector

How To Play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals displayed below. Click on them to add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win if the total score matches the random number.

You lose if the score goes above the random number.

The values of the crystals and random number will change each time the game restarts.
